Description

Grilled chicken with romaine, corn, beans, tomatoes, and onions with creamy avocado ranch dressing.

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado - peeled and chopped
  • 1/4 Sour Cream
  • 1 tbsp mayo
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 2 tbsp squeezed lime juice
  • 1/2 packet ranch dressing mix
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast cut in halves
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 1/2 tbsp fajita seasoning
  • 1 medium ear corn, husk and silk removed
  • 1 medium romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup halved grape tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can black beans
  • 1/2 small red onion, sliced into petals

Steps

  1. Mix avocado, sour cream, mayonnaise, water, lime juice, ranch seasoning, and salt in a food processor until smooth. Refrigerate, we will use later.
  2. Place the chicken in a bowl. Mix olive oil and fajita seasoning
  3. Set grill temperature to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate. Add chicken and corn.
